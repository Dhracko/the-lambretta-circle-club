The Lambretta Circle

The Lambretta Circle is a club based in the heart of the costwold in Gloucestershire. We ride around the local villages and towns.


We meet three times a year for locals food samples making the venue an opportunity for those heart loving Lambretta owners to meet up and share their passion and enthusiastic bikes


Feel free to leave your details and any comment you would like to tell us or if you want more information about the Club.
